# Privacy Policy for Kadima **Last Updated: May 1, 2026** Welcome to Kadima ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure spiritual experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Kadima mobile application. ## 1. Information We Collect ### A. Personal Information When you use Kadima, we may collect certain personal information that you provide to us, including: * **Account Information**: Your name and email address provided through "Sign In with Apple." * **Profile Information**: Any display name or preferences you set within the app. ### B. Purchase and Subscription Data We use **RevenueCat** to manage our subscription services. When you make a purchase: * Transaction details (date, amount, product) are processed by Apple. * We receive a unique identifier to verify your subscription status. We do not see or store your credit card information. ### C. Usage Data and Content * **AI Interactions**: We collect the text you enter during prayer reflections and AI mentorship sessions to provide personalized spiritual guidance. This data is processed by our AI providers (e.g., DeepSeek/OpenAI via Supabase) to generate responses. * **App Usage**: We may collect technical data such as device type, OS version, and app interaction logs to improve performance. ## 2. How We Use Your Information We use the collected information to: * Provide and maintain the Kadima spiritual experience. * Personalize your growth journey and AI mentorship. * Manage your "kadima Pro" subscription. * Communicate with you regarding app updates or support requests. ## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted service providers necessary for app functionality: * **Apple**: For authentication (Sign In with Apple) and payment processing. * **RevenueCat**: For managing subscription logic and entitlements. * **Supabase/AI Providers**: For hosting our backend and processing AI-driven spiritual reflections. ## 4. Your Rights and Choices * **Data Access & Deletion**: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data directly within the app settings. * **Subscription Management**: You can cancel or modify your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings. ## 5. Security We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. ## 6. Children's Privacy Kadima is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ## 7.
