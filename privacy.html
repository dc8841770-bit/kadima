Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

Welcome to Kadima. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: We collect only what is necessary to run the app. We do not sell your prayer reflections, and our AI partner operates on a Zero-Data-Retention policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: We collect your name and email via Sign In with Apple to authenticate your account and sync your progress.
  • Sensitive Religious Data: As a prayer journal and Bible study tool, the reflections and prayers you enter are considered sensitive personal data. These are encrypted and stored securely.
  • AI Prompts: Text sent to our AI assistant is used to generate scripture-based mentorship.
  • Usage & Diagnostics: We collect anonymous crash logs and feature interaction data to improve the app performance.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted third-party providers to power specific app features:

  • Apple: Provides secure authentication and payment processing.
  • Adapty: Manages your subscription status, paywalls, and receipts.
  • Supabase: Provides our encrypted database and cloud edge functions (like Text-To-Speech).
  • SiliconFlow (AI Engine): Processes your AI questions. SiliconFlow operates under a Zero Data Retention policy for Kadima; your prompts are not stored or used to train their models.

3. AI Accuracy & Safety Disclaimer

Kadima uses Artificial Intelligence to provide spiritual guidance. Please be aware:

  • The AI is an algorithm, not a human mentor, and should not replace professional spiritual or mental health counseling.
  • AI-generated content may occasionally be inaccurate. Users are encouraged to verify all suggestions against the included Bible text.

4. Data Rights & Deletion

In accordance with global privacy standards (including GDPR, CCPA, and the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act), you have the right to:

  • Request Access: View or export the data we have stored for you.
  • Data Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated journal entries at any time within the App Settings. Once deleted, your data is purged from our servers within 30 days.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit and encryption at rest for your personal journal entries within Supabase.
